Bloomreach and the Brandfolder DAM integration

Recently Bloomreach Content released the feature: “Custom Integrations” in which integrations with 3rd party vendors such as DAM systems can be achieved by using an App framework in the Bloomreach Content.

Brandfolder is one of our preferred DAM systems and a strong technology partner. With the Brandfolder integration, CMS editors can search and place assets from Brandfolder DAM to a website managed by the Bloomreach Experience Manager
